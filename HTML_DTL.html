Technical Limit Curve

Lower Lance Horizontal Program

These composite curves are a barometer of technological advances, improvements in processes of our drilling program and satisfy the primary functions of the DWOP philosophy: Set the standard, facilitate collective engagement with data, and track progress. After completing the first 20 wells as part of the nascent Pinedale horizontal drilling. program in 2018, this Lower Lance Technical Limit well was built to show the potential of the program once all the best practices and lessons learned were standardized. Had the horizontal program continued, the role of the DWOP coordinator would be to continue to push performance ever closer to that curve. The key to reaching these goals is setting aggressive, attainable targets and revisiting performance metrics at DWOP meetings to facilitate planning for efficiencies.
