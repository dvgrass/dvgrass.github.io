Downhole Motor Reliability

Stator Tests and Clearance Fit Standards

Through 2017, Ultra ran seven different slim hole motors with nine different elastomers and no clearance fit standards. By the end of 2019, those numbers were whittled down to predominately two motor vendors that both used the highest performing elastomer and an approved fit clearance. A ‘Probability of DMF versus Run Length (hrs.)’ curve was generated in the 6” runs DB to identify the longest-lasting motors. As lower performing motors were culled from selection, there was an acute improvement in the percentage of 3-bit wells drilled quarter over quarter.
