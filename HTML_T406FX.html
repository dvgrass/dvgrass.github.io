Baker Hughes Production Drill Out Runs

Cutter Brazing Issues for Re-Runs

Brazing issues on shoulder cutters were identified as part of an audit on serial numbers for Baker Hughe's produciton bits. Cutters were exiting clean out of the pockets on most second runs, a tell-tale sign that incorrect powders were being used in matrix rebuilds. As a result of this study, specific part numbers were limited to one run until manufacturing changes were implemented.
