DWOP Program

Drilling Data Analytics for Ultra Petroleum on the Pinedale Anticline

High Level Metrics

The DWOP (‘Drill Well on Paper’) Program was integrated into Ultra Petroleum in 2006 as a drilling initiative from Mike Cooney and Kent Rogers. The DWOP program’s processes and framework have since been integral in establishing Ultra’s performance-driven culture. Long term drilling statistics and feedback from vendors, consultants, and contractors all demonstrate the program’s success. Ultra has designated personnel who deliver outcomes of excellent drilling performances and drilling recap that have documented and propagated continuous drilling improvement.

What distinguishes Ultra’s DWOP program from other drilling analysis platforms are the ways in which drilling data and a vast sampling of KPIs are shared, reviewed, and dissected collaboratively with every individual on a rig-site location.
